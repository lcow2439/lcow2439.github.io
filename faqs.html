Frequently Asked Questions

What is Harmonicade?

Harmonicade is a revolutionary outdoor audio system that blends seamlessly into your garden or backyard, delivering powerful sound and weather-resistant design.

How do I set up Harmonicade?

Setting up Harmonicade is easy! Simply follow the instructions provided in the user manual included with your purchase.

Can I connect Harmonicade to my existing audio system?

Yes, Harmonicade is compatible with most audio systems. You can easily connect it to your existing setup for enhanced sound quality.

Is Harmonicade waterproof?

Yes, Harmonicade is designed to withstand outdoor conditions, including rain and snow. Its weather-resistant construction ensures durability and long-lasting performance.
